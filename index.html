From Players to Teams: Performance Trends and Aging Patterns in Baseball History

This project explores how age, skill, and team dynamics shape performance in Major League Baseball. We examine star hitters and pitchers by era, analyze how team age affects success, and track league performance trends over time using interactive visualizations.

Write-Up

What have we done so far?

We created three interactive visualizations using D3.js to analyze baseball performance from both player and team perspectives. One visualization explores top hitters and pitchers across eras, another shows how team age relates to performance, and a third tracks league metrics such as scoring, hits, and strikeouts over time. These charts include filters, tooltips, and animations so users can explore data dynamically.

What will be most challenging?

The biggest challenge is keeping interactions intuitive while combining multiple datasets and perspectives. Coordinating filters across metrics, leagues, and years while keeping visuals readable and responsive requires careful design. Another challenge is ensuring that performance trends remain clear even with overlapping team and league lines.
