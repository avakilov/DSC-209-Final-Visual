From Players to Teams: Performance Trends and Aging Patterns in Baseball History

This project explores how age, skill, and team structure shape performance in Major League Baseball. We compare star hitters and pitchers across eras, analyze team age and success, and track long-term league trends using interactive visualizations.

Do Older Baseball Teams Perform Better?

We examine how average team age relates to performance using runs per game and win percentage from 1970–2015.

Write-Up

What have we done so far?

We created three interactive D3 visualizations: player performance by era, team age vs performance, and league trends over time. The charts support exploration through filters, tooltips, and animations.

What will be most challenging?

Coordinating interactions across multiple datasets and keeping visual clarity while supporting user exploration will be the most difficult challenge.
